Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Text Tools.

What does the Word Counter tool count?

It counts words, characters (with and without spaces), sentences, paragraphs, reading time, speaking time, estimated pages, most repeated word, and keyword density.

How does the Case Converter work?

Simply paste your text, select a conversion option (UPPERCASE, lowercase, Title Case, etc.), and click Convert. The tool will transform your text instantly.

What is Lorem Ipsum?

Lorem Ipsum is placeholder text commonly used in the design and publishing industries. It allows designers to focus on layout without being distracted by meaningful content.

Is my data stored anywhere?

No. All text processing happens locally in your browser. Your data never leaves your device. The Word Counter saves your text in Local Storage for convenience.

Can I export my text?

Yes. Each tool has a "Download TXT" button that lets you save your text as a .txt file. You can also copy text to your clipboard with one click.

Are these tools free?

Yes, all Text Tools are 100% free to use. No signup, no limits, no hidden fees.
