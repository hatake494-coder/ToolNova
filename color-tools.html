Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about using Color Tools.

What color formats are supported?

Color Tools supports HEX, RGB, and HSL formats. You can convert between any of these formats instantly.

How do I copy a color value?

Click the "Copy" button next to any color value. A toast notification will confirm that the value has been copied to your clipboard.

How does the random color generator work?

Click "Generate Random" to get a completely random color. You can also generate pastel colors or dark colors with the dedicated buttons.

Where are my recent colors saved?

Recent colors are saved in your browser's local storage. They will persist even after you close the page. Click any recent color to load it instantly.

Can I use these colors for my design projects?

Absolutely! All colors generated or picked are standard web colors that you can use in CSS, design software, or any creative project.

Is this tool free?

Yes, Color Tools is completely free to use. No signup, no limits, no hidden fees.
