Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about the Password Generator.

Is this password generator secure?

Yes. All password generation happens entirely in your browser using JavaScript's built-in crypto API. No passwords are ever sent to any server.

How is password strength measured?

Strength is measured using entropy (bits of randomness), estimated crack time, and a security score. The meter updates in real-time as you adjust settings.

What is the difference between the generator modes?

Random generates completely random passwords. Pronounceable creates easy-to-read but secure passwords. Passphrase generates memorable word combinations. PIN creates numeric codes.

Are my passwords saved anywhere?

Passwords are saved locally in your browser's storage for history and favorites. They never leave your device and can be cleared at any time.

Can I export my generated passwords?

Yes. You can download all generated passwords as a TXT file or copy them all to your clipboard with one click.

Is there a limit on password length?

The generator supports lengths from 4 to 128 characters, giving you flexibility for any use case.
